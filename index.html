About Us

Jack and Jill English Medium School

Jack and Jill English Medium School is a dedicated educational institution located in Ghule Vasti, Hadapsar, Pune, committed to nurturing young learners through a structured and engaging English medium curriculum. Our school provides a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment where children develop not only academically but also socially and personally. We believe every child deserves the best possible start in life, and our dedicated staff work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Conveniently situated on Hadapsar Road, our school serves the local community with a focus on building strong foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. We operate Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, ensuring structured learning hours that balance education with rest. Whether you are looking for a school that values discipline, creativity, or holistic development, Jack and Jill English Medium School is the right choice for your child's bright future.

  • English medium instruction for strong language foundations
  • Conveniently located on Hadapsar Road, Pune
  • Structured school hours Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 3 PM
